知行 — 隐私政策

生效日期：2026年3月23日 | 最后更新：2026年3月23日

一、概述

知行（以下简称「本应用」）是一款基于梅花易数的传统文化学习工具。我们高度重视用户隐私，本政策说明我们如何处理您的信息。

二、数据收集与存储

本应用不收集、不传输、不存储任何个人数据到远程服务器。所有数据均存储在您的设备本地：

  • 学习记录：保存在设备本地存储（UserDefaults），仅限本设备访问
  • 主题偏好：深色/浅色模式设置，存储在设备本地
  • 使用频率：学习频率记录，存储在设备本地

三、第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

内购功能由 Apple StoreKit 框架提供，相关交易由 Apple 处理，请参阅 Apple 隐私政策。

四、剪贴板使用

本应用仅在您主动点击「复制」按钮时访问系统剪贴板，用于复制学习内容摘要或 AI 提示词。我们不会在后台读取或监控剪贴板内容。

五、数据删除

您可以随时在应用「设置」页面中清空学习记录或重置所有数据。卸载应用将自动删除所有本地数据。

六、儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

七、政策变更

如本政策有更新，我们将在应用内发布新版本并更新生效日期。

ZhiXing — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026 | Last Updated: March 23, 2026

1. Overview

ZhiXing ("the App") is a traditional Chinese culture learning tool based on Plum Blossom Numerology (Meihua Yishu). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection & Storage

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on remote servers. All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Learning records: Saved in local device storage (UserDefaults), accessible only on this device
  • Theme preferences: Dark/light mode settings, stored locally
  • Usage frequency: Learning frequency records, stored locally

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

In-app purchases are powered by Apple StoreKit. Related transactions are handled by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Clipboard Usage

The App accesses the system clipboard only when you actively tap the "Copy" button, to copy learning content summaries or AI prompts. We do not read or monitor clipboard content in the background.

5. Data Deletion

You can clear learning records or reset all data at any time from the "Settings" page. Uninstalling the App will automatically delete all local data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Policy Changes

If this policy is updated, we will post the new version on this page and update the effective date.
